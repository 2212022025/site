Event Rules & Regulation

Attendance & Combat

  • Attendance: Players must join the game at their scheduled match time.
  • Combat: Players are required to compete against their opponent using designated in-game abilities.

Scoring System

  • Win: 2 points.
  • Loss: 0 points.
  • No Join: -0.5 points (if a player fails to join).

Code Security & Rule

  • Do not attempt to join the match before your scheduled time.
  • Never share your room code with others.
  • All match codes will be delivered exclusively via the app or your registered email address.

Opponent No Join Policy

  • If your opponent fails to join the match, you must wait for exactly five minutes.
  • If they have not arrived by the end of that period, you may leave the game.
  • Example: If your start time is 8:40 AM, wait until 8:46 AM before leaving.
